Incident Summary:
On the night of April 12th, a residential burglary occurred at the home of James and Linda Peterson at 215 Oak Tree Lane. The victims reported that unknown suspect(s) forcibly gained entry through a rear kitchen window by breaking the latch lock using lock picking tools. Once inside, the suspect(s) ransacked the first floor of the home, rifling through drawers, cabinets and closets. 

The Peterson family, including two children ages 8 and 10, were asleep upstairs during the incident. Mrs. Peterson was awoken around 2:15 AM by noises coming from downstairs. Upon investigating, she encountered an intruder in the hallway near the staircase who then physically assaulted her, shoving her to the floor, before fleeing out the broken kitchen window.

- Small set of lockpicking tools found on kitchen floor near window

- Disarray of household items/contents strewn about downstairs rooms
- Mrs. Peterson sustained minor bruising/abrasions from being shoved to the floor
