- A 55-inch Samsung LED TV
- An Apple MacBook Pro laptop
- A Nikon D850 DSLR camera with lenses
- Assorted jewelry from Mrs. Martin's collection
- Mr. Martin's wallet containing cash and credit cards

No signs of violence or physical altercation were observed at the scene. The victims were unharmed during the incident, as they were asleep upstairs when the burglary occurred.

Forensic teams were dispatched to collect evidence, including fingerprints and any potential DNA traces left behind by the perpetrator(s). Neighbors in the vicinity were interviewed, but no one reported seeing or hearing any suspicious activity during the time of the incident.

The investigation is ongoing, and the Oakwood City Police Department is actively pursuing leads to identify and apprehend the individual(s) responsible for this burglary. Residents in the area are advised to remain vigilant and report any suspicious activity immediately.
