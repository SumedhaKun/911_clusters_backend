POLICE INCIDENT REPORT
Case No: 2023-04718

Crime Type: Burglary 

On April 15th, 2023 at approximately 2:30 AM, officers responded to a residential burglary call at 215 Oak Tree Lane. Upon arrival, they found signs of forced entry through a rear window of the home.

The victims, John and Jane Doe, reported that they were awoken by loud noises coming from the kitchen area. Mr. Doe went to investigate and encountered an intruder, described as a male around 6 feet tall wearing a black ski mask, dark clothing, and gloves. 

The suspect gained entry by removing the window screen from the kitchen window and prying it open using his bare hands and brute force. No tools were utilized to access the secured location.

When confronted by Mr. Doe, the suspect violently shoved him to the ground and proceeded to ransack the home, taking various electronics, jewelry, and cash. The type of force used was physical assault and intimidation through the threat of bodily harm.

No weapons were brandished or used during the commission of this crime.

Evidence collected at the scene includes fingerprints on the window frame, shoeprints, and DNA evidence from the suspect's discarded gloves and mask found in the backyard.

The suspect remains at large, and the investigation is ongoing. Anyone with information is urged to contact the police department's tip line.
